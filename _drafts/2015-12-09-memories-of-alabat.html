Backstory

After working abroad for over returned years, my mother came back in 1990. It wasn’t a pleasant affair because by the time her 2 week vacation was over my father left us, never to return. My mother decided to “hide” us from my father that is why we ended up in Alabat, Quezon.

Barangay Tres

My uncle is a local in Alabat. He helped us settle down. By the time we arrived there our would be home is still being constructed so we had to rent a house. This house was located in Barangay Tres.
